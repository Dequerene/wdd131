Culture and Food

Mozambique has a rich culture shaped by many ethnic groups, languages, music, dance, and traditions.

Culture

Visitors can experience local hospitality, traditional markets, handmade crafts, music, dance, and cultural celebrations. Greeting people politely is an important part of daily life.

Food

Mozambican food is known for seafood, rice, cassava, corn, coconut, peanuts, and spicy flavors. Popular dishes include matapa, grilled fish, xima, and peri-peri chicken.
