Mozambique Travel and Culture Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Mozambique Travel and Culture Guide

This name was chosen because the website will help visitors learn about Mozambique’s travel destinations, culture, food, and traditions.

Optional domain: mozambiquetravelguide.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide helpful information about Mozambique, including tourist attractions, cultural traditions, local food, travel tips, and beautiful places to visit.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Mozambique?
  • What traditional foods should I try in Mozambique?
  • What cultural customs should visitors know before traveling?
  • When is the best time to visit Mozambique?
