Mozambique Travel and Culture Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Mozambique Travel and Culture Guide

I chose this name because it clearly describes the purpose of the website. The site will help visitors learn about Mozambique’s culture, tourism, food, traditions, and travel opportunities.

Optional Domain: mozambiquetravelguide.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about Mozambique's tourist attractions, culture, traditional foods, travel tips, and important destinations. Visitors will be able to learn about the country and plan future visits.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular tourist attractions in Mozambique?
  • What traditional foods should visitors try?
  • What cultural customs should travelers know before visiting?
  • When is the best time of year to visit Mozambique?
